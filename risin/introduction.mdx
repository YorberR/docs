# 🇻🇪 RISIN System

**Ministry of Indigenous Peoples (MINPI) — Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela**

RISIN (*Registro de Inclusión Social Indígena*) is a comprehensive technological tool developed for the social management teams at MINPI. Its primary purpose is to register, monitor, report, and evaluate the effectiveness of support programs dedicated to indigenous communities.

## 🏗️ System Overview

Built with modern PHP architecture, RISIN acts as the single source of truth for social deployments. It handles massive amounts of demographic and geographic data, linking it directly to state-sponsored social missions and specific benefits.

### Key Capabilities
* **Demographic Registry:** Tracks citizens, their specific vulnerabilities (e.g., malnutrition, homelessness), and their affiliations with indigenous communities.
* **Geographic Engine:** Maps data down to the exact state, municipality, parish, and specific community.
* **Benefit Distribution:** Logs exactly *what* benefit was delivered, *who* received it, and *when*, ensuring total transparency.
* **Real-time Analytics:** Features a powerful dashboard for directors to make data-driven decisions based on live field reports.
